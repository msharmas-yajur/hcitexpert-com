These are the most often mentioned aspects of Healthcare, that needs to be addressed by not only the government, but also the Startup community willing to disrupt the Health Tech / Digital Health industry. </span></span></span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">I have attempted to share my thoughts on Connected Care questions put forward during the tweetchat and I hope you will consider <b><i>sharing your insights by filling in the form below</i></b></span></span></span></span><br /><h1 style="line-height:1.56;margin-bottom:0;margin-top:24pt;orphans:2;text-align:justify;text-decoration-color:initial;text-decoration-style:initial;text-indent:0;widows:2;"><span><span style="font-size:24px;">1. Is the healthcare industry ready to embrace connected care?</span></span></h1><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">In India, with the major push for digital services by the govt and private healthcare facilities, and with 350+MN internet users connected care is the only way to solve the accessibility to healthcare problem (1:3200 doctor to patient ratio)</span></span><br /><h1 style="line-height:1.56;margin-bottom:0;margin-top:24pt;"><span><span style="font-size:24px;">4. Based on your experience, what are the elements to enable connected care further?</span></span></h1><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">The connected care needs to bring about change in thought of how to use a connected care framework for the patient as well as the doctor. </span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><b>For the patient, connected care is about </b></span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- experience that enables an ease of access to care</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- Ability to build their own healthcare record’s completeness </span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- Have a better set of processes and #workflows to manage their health and care </span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- Have the ability to find "patients like me" and be part of the community </span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><b>For the Doctor, I believe it will be about </b></span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- how to glean new insights from the data stream</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- How to collaborate with a patient via an always connected model? What signifies the end of a consultation? </span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- To build constantly evolving care plans for their patients, based on realtime, near-realtime, time-delay, or frequency per day/week month updates</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- To evolve more treatment plans based on the insights that can be drawn from the raw patient data feed (an e.g.)</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">- How to build a community and be part of a community of specialists to keep themselves up-to-date on the current research and practices.</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><span><br /></span></span></span> <br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">I am including the Questions as a Google Form, do consider sharing your insights into what is Connected Care?
