Remote patient monitoring technologies would be just a small cog in the wheel of Connected health. Health and fitness monitoring will precede patient monitoring as the driver for IoT solutions in healthcare. IoT in healthcare holds great promise for the coming generations because it could just transform the quality of life of the aging population.
