It is creating new channels for improving patient experience, creating better clinical processes and engaging doctors and other para-clinical staff like never before.</i></span></blockquote><div dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-2a068dbc-fe6e-999c-4605-22a11f0a3f6b" style="line-height:2.0736;margin-bottom:0;margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;"><hr /></div><div dir="ltr" style="margin-bottom:0;margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;"><div style="line-height:2.0736;"><div dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-7f8576b5-ff00-784f-b097-8486d6015797" style="line-height:1.8;margin-bottom:10pt;margin-top:10pt;text-align:justify;"><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">It is also creating new models for areas like medical education, rehabilitation and managing supply chain. In these there key areas Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) are playing a key role. But first let’s start with the definition and differences between AR and VR.</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"></span></span><br /><a name='more'></a><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><b>Augmented Reality or AR</b> is a live direct or indirect view of a physical, real-world environment whose elements are "augmented" by computer-generated sensory input such as sound, video, graphics or GPS data. So the game Pokemon Go is a good example of AR. (Source: Wikipedia)</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">While VR on the other hand <b>VR- Virtual reality (VR)</b> is a technology that uses Virtual reality headsets, sometimes in combination with physical spaces or multi-projected environments, to generate realistic images, sounds and other sensations that simulate a user's physical presence in a virtual or imaginary environment. (Source: Wikipedia) So VR requires a headset specialized for the same. </span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">The Industry seems to be taking notice of this emerging area. These are some numbers from the industry</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">1. More than 150 of Global Fortune 500 investing in AR/VR</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">2. VC and corporate investment upto $2.3 Billion in AR/VR Startups </span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">3. IDC projects revenues from AR/VR to grow from $5.2 billion today to $162 billion by 2020</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">But why are the key reasons why AR/VR has reached this level</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">1. We have more computing power than ever before. Today we have GPU’s and TPU’s that put immense computing power at our disposal</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">2. There is a explosion in digital data. By 2020 it is believed that we would have created 40,000 Exabyte of data</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">3. Finally programmers today are writing better algorithms. That is why machine learning is mainstream today</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">So what are the implications of the same in healthcare?</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">There are clearly implications in Medical Education. Pilots conducted in Miami Children’s hospital have shown an 80% increase in retention while using AR/VR. There are other pilots involving treating war veterans using VR for Post Traumatic Stress Disorder.</span></span><br /><span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;"><br /></span></span> <span><span style="font-size:18.6667px;">I will be speaking on this topic on, 23 Aug 2017, at the <a href="http://ith-india.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">IT Healthcare Summit 2017</a> in Bangalore.
