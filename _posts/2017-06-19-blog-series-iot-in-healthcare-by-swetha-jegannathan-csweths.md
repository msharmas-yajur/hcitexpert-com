Geriatric Care - IoT is and will continue to be of great value in elderly care, allowing the doctors and care givers to monitor, track and alert when away from their loved ones - especially in cases of neurological disorders like Dementia and Alzheimer. </span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span>2. Maternal and Infant Monitoring - IoT, through monitoring devices worn by the individual, can provide timely intervention in the area of maternal and infant health - one of the primary goals of the UN sustainable development agenda.</span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span>3. Hospitals would be the drivers providing this service to their patients with companies engaged in the manufacture and distribution of medical devices being the enablers of the technology.</span></div><div dir="ltr" style="line-height:1.8;margin-bottom:10pt;margin-top:10pt;text-align:justify;"><br /></div><div dir="ltr" style="line-height:1.8;margin-bottom:10pt;margin-top:10pt;text-align:justify;"><span>Q3. Standards?: </span></div><div dir="ltr" style="line-height:1.8;margin-bottom:10pt;margin-top:10pt;text-align:justify;"><span>Swetha Jegannathan: </span><span>RFID based devices connected through the anti-collision protocols and the Apple watch will be the future.</span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span>The RFID chips are inserted into the human beings for unique identification and capture of information relating to their general health and well being like blood pressure, weight, blood sugar levels.  The RFID tags can also be used for improving the efficiency of the medicine distribution networks as tracking of the medicines becomes easier.</span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span>Prevention of inappropriate usage of the collected information in terms of charging higher insurance premiums or classification of the individuals tracked by the device is one of the biggest challenges to its widespread implementation. Further, a standard medical data code for data transmission and retrieval is a prerequisite. </span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span>Once the data is collected, stored and retrieved efficiently, analysis is accurate and easier leading to appropriate decision making. </span></div><div dir="ltr" style="line-height:1.8;margin-bottom:10pt;margin-top:10pt;text-align:justify;"><br /></div><div dir="ltr" style="line-height:1.8;margin-bottom:10pt;margin-top:10pt;text-align:justify;"><span>Q5. Preventive health - with regular check-ups, timely alerts and early interventions</span><span><br class="kix-line-break" /></span><span>2.
