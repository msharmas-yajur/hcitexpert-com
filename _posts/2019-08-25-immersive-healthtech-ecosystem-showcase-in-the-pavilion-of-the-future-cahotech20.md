<h2>So, what is CAHOTech 2019?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><br><em>CAHO is Consortium of Accredited Healthcare Organisations</em>. This is the fourth year they are conducting &nbsp;CAHOTech, an event of a one-day format with speakers in a conference and stalls by participants.&nbsp;<br><br><strong><a href="http://cahotech.com/venue.php" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Venue: IIT Madras Research Park</a></strong><br><strong>Date: September 28</strong><br><br>This year, there is a small twist to how we are featuring the companies at this Conference.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>A Brief history of HealthTech Connect-a-thons in India</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>There is a group of us who have had good success in showcasing the capabilities of HealthTech companies here in India. The first in the series was a Testathon conducted at Philips, Bangalore in 2012. The next was a <a href="https://blog.hcitexpert.com/p/connectathon-2016.html" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Connect-A-thon at St. John’s, Bangalore in 2016</a>. The third in line was an Interoperability Showcase at the Medicall Conference in Chennai in the year 2017.&nbsp;<br><br>This year CAHO - 2019 carries the torch.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>So What's the Difference in this years Showcase:&nbsp;The "Pavilion&nbsp;of the Future"</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The stakeholders in this conference are investors, hospital administrators (CEOs, CTOs, CxOs), physicians, other medical experts and students. To make it meaningful for them, we will be showcasing about 40 companies through “scenarios”. Each scenario while exhibiting specific industry functionality will also highlight four common “competitive parity” themes&nbsp;<br><br><strong><em>- Customer Experience,&nbsp;</em></strong><br><strong><em>- Care Delivery,&nbsp;</em></strong><br><strong><em>- Operational Excellence and&nbsp;</em></strong><br><strong><em>- Quality</em></strong><br><br>Additionally, we will also showcase industry solutions for two or three Public Health problems in India. This will be futuristic solutions.&nbsp;<br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h2>What does the The CAHOTech 2019 Immersive Experience have for me as a Startup, as an Investor?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><br>HealthTech companies use technology (databases, applications, mobiles, wearables) to improve the delivery, payment, and/or consumption of care, with the ability to increase the development and commercialization of medicinal products.<br><br>Stakeholders consist of hospitals and practitioners; insurance; consumer-facing services; pharmaceuticals; and government.&nbsp;<br><br>The Healthcare industry was valued at US$7.2 trillion in 2015 in the US alone.<br><br>In India where the market is huge in numbers and requires innovation to deliver at appropriate price points, the startup community is embracing the challenge.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><br><strong>For the Startups:&nbsp;</strong><br>To showcase what is happening in the Ecosystem, CAHOTech 2019 is providing a platform for startups to exhibit what they do and how they do it in interoperable ways with other companies for the stakeholders. The exhibits will allow the delegate visitor to experience the flow of data and triggers that initiate functionality offered by individual companies in an immersive way through role plays called scenarios.&nbsp;<br><br>These scenarios are designed to illustrate real life situations happening at point of care and the mechanics involved to ensure the delivery of care to the users of the various systems.&nbsp;<br><br>Over and above the core solutions that companies provide to their clients, they also design their systems to ensure a high level of Customer Experience that are delivered in the right manner at the right time for the client at optimal performances. These hidden strengths of the systems designed by the startups will be brought to the fore for the visitor to experience for themselves.&nbsp;<br><br>You will have access to a monitor in your station (we will call it station instead of stall). The scenarios will ensure that you will be able to explain your solution's functionality through a story. What that will mean is, you will present on-screen to the delegates and do your show-and-tell with the gadgets, apps, solutions, demos as well.<br></p>
