And hence we came up with the "Paperless Hospital" approach.<br /><br />But the flaw in the paperless approach, in my opinion is the approach that caused the creation of Information silos. We Digitised the Paper, and not the workflow.<br /><br />Take for instance the workflow of a Doctor in a hospital.
