<ul><li><strong>Directions to View the MindMap&nbsp;</strong><em>(best viewed on a laptop/ desktop):&nbsp;</em> </li><li>[1]: The mind map comes with a <strong><em>zoom-in zoom-out buttons on the top right</em></strong> of the mind map. You can also <strong><em>click on any of the nodes</em></strong>, once the node is selected,&nbsp;<strong><em>You can toggle the left side panel at any time by pressing `Tab` on your keyboard or clicking the three gray dots to the right of the panel.</em></strong>&nbsp; </li><li>[2]: On a laptop or desktop, when you select a node, you can also <strong><em>click on the TAB button to view / hide the information pane</em></strong> on the left hand side of the mind map </li><li>[3]: You can <strong><em>search for specific terms</em></strong> and the mind map will show you the nodes that reference that particular term. [4]: <strong><em>Mouse over a node (on a desktop/laptop)</em></strong> shows you the mapping to the related nodes &nbsp; </li></ul>
