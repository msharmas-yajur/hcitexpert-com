<h2>Behind the Scenes</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>ZINI</strong> can help every player involved in the healthcare sector from patients to healthcare professionals. ZINI can help with increasing efficacy, gathering clinical data, promoting research and making data driven policies.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li><strong>Discuss your symptoms:</strong> Why google your symptoms? When you can share them with <strong>ZINI</strong> and do an active 2 way communication. Share what bothers you with her and she will start a thorough patient interview like a human physician.</li><li><strong>Get the right advice: </strong>When there is no expert available at hand, people make do with whatever is available. They go to a chemist for medical advice or worse, a quack with no medical degree. Delay or misinformation can lead to fatal consequences. In order to end this crisis in the healthcare sector, we invented <strong>ZINI</strong>. Share your symptoms with <strong>ZINI</strong> and get the right advice in a timely manner.</li><li><strong>Available in Indian Languages</strong> <strong>ZINI</strong> can now talk to patients in <strong>Hindi, Punjabi and Bangla as well as English</strong>. More languages are being added in time.</li></ul>
